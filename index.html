Summary

Transformers Prime is about how Optimus Prime and his other Autobot companions hid away on earth because their home planet, Cyberton was destroyed. When humans found them, they had to learn to cooperate and eventually work together with them.

Soon enough, Megatron, who was thought to be dead after Cyberton exploded, seemed to be alive and well. Megatron's plan is to terraform the Earth to be exactly like Cyberton. Optimus and the Autobots, as well as the humans, have to stop him before it is too late.

My Opinion

In my opinion, Transformers Prime was an amazing show. The first two seasons were by far the best seasons. After season 2, the show kinda lost it's touch a little bit. I wasn't as interested before. But overall, I would rate it a 7.5/10
